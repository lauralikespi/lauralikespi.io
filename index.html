We’re taught by humans, a social enterprise aimed at making technology education more fair. We think education needs some innovation, and we want to make sure everyone is included.

We want to run courses and initiatives to ensure everybody has the chance to learn or reskill, particularly as technology advances. The biggest skill we want instill in our students is learning how to learn. We not only want to teach the contents of our curriculums - we want to help everyone learn for themselves.

We want to build a community that provides opportunities to our members. Creating our own version of nepotism.

We value teaching. We don’t have teachers, who just “teach”. We have developers, innovators, designers, roboticists, the list goes on and on, who want to pass on what they love to others.

We have lots more to say about education, technology, innovation in our thoughts by humans blog. If you'd like some more information, download our one-pager here or contact us

Happy Learning :)
