Next Level

Background

We participated in STEM Game Changers, a hackathon to address diversity in STEM. Our idea Next Level - a new sustainable, long-term mentoring scheme - won a runner up prize!

How do kids normally find out about careers? Often they are limited to the careers their parents and teachers know of.

However, there are infinite numbers of careers and this mentoring scheme aims to expose kids to a range of careers they might not normally hear of

We are going to have a kick off event, to match kids to mentors. Then the children will drive the mentoring scheme - do they want work experience, or maybe they want a research project?

Pilot

We want this to be a nationwide scheme, but our pilot will take place in London.

We're being ambitious and aiming for the end of November.

So if you're interested in signing up as a mentor, please let us know.

Or if you'd like to register a child (or children)
